Your privacy matters.

This Privacy Policy explains how Pilot Fuel Calculator (“the App”) handles your information. The App is designed to work offline and does not require you to create an account.

✓ Simple promise: We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • No account registration.
  • No analytics tracking.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No location access.
  • No contact or photo access.
! The calculations you enter (fuel values, weights, notes) stay on your device.

How the App Works

The App performs fuel planning calculations locally on your device. Any data you type is processed only to show results and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Storage

If the App offers saving presets/history, that information is stored locally on your device. You can remove it by deleting the App or using any in-app reset option (if available).

Security

Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no server-side storage of your information.
